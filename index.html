How to play:

  • You are a green snake.
  • You start as a speck of a snake.
  • Your tail will grow as you eat oranges.
  • Your goal is to eat as many oranges as you can.
  • You get a point every time you eat an orange.
  • Use arrow keys to move yourself.
  • There’s no turning back.
  • There’s no turning on yourself.
  • Do not get off the grid.
  • If you try going anywhere that’s not an empty space or just an orange, you will lose.
  • There’s no winning in this game, only losing.
  • If you came here to win, you will be disappointed.
  • If you came here to eat oranges, you will not be disappointed.
